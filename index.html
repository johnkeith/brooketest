You can get back on track, and I'm here to help.

Whether you're looking for specific skills, a place to sort out your thoughts and feelings or some professional recommendations and feedback, I have the training to help with a variety of concerns. My philosophy is to treat the whole person - not just the feelings of depression, anxiety, or problems in your life you may be struggling with. I want you to have long-lasting results that you take with you as your life moves forward after therapy. The best part of my job is getting you to the point when you know that you don't need my help anymore. I want you to fire me someday!.

As a licensed professional counselor, I work with clients who are age 15 and older. I specialize in anxiety and stress management, though I work with a range of other concerns. In addition to individual counseling, I also offer premarital counseling and marriage counseling focusing on building communication skills, reducing conflict, improving trust and rebuilding from infidelity. If you're considering counseling, feel free to give me a call to talk more about what you're looking for and explore whether I'm a good fit for you. I love the work that I do and welcome the chance to talk about it with others - please don't hesitate to contact me with questions.

Specializing in Anxiety and Stress Management

I help people with many different kinds of challenges. My speciality is helping clients with levels of stress or anxiety that interfere with enjoying their life. Some common examples include work or school stress, social anxiety, the stress of being a people-pleaser or perfectionist, anxiety following trauma or abuse and existential anxiety for those questioning their life path.

Counseling

Many mental health concerns can be resolved in 6-12 sessions. It's best to try to meet 3-4 times per month in the beginning stages of counseling. Some conditions lend themselves more to long-term counseling, though we may see each other less frequently over time. You and I can discuss together what length and frequency of sessions would suit you best.
